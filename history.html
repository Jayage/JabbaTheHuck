Jabba the Huck is Ireland's oldest Ultimate Frisbee club, established in Dublin in 2008. Based in North Dublin Jabba is a Mixed club, with men and women playing and socialising together. We're a diverse group of ages and experiences and welcome anyone who wants to come and play. Jabba are also the current All Ireland Mixed Champions and have won the title a total of 4 times in 2012, 2013, 2017, and 2018.
What is Frisbee

Ultimate frisbee is a fast paced team sport. Comprised of 7 players on each team. the objective is to pass the disc between their team mates to catch the disc in a designated endzone.
